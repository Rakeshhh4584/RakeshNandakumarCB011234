Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Welcome to Kosgoda Turtle Hatcheries


Experience the wonder of sea turtles at Kosgoda Turtle Hatcheries. Our conservation program is dedicated to protecting and preserving these magnificent creatures for generations to come.

About Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a non-profit organization that aims to protect sea turtles and their eggs. The hatchery is located on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka and is dedicated to preserving the five species of sea turtles that come to nest on the beaches. The hatchery collects turtle eggs and hatches them in a protected area. When the hatchlings are ready, they are released into the sea. Visitors to the hatchery can learn about the different turtle species and witness the release of the baby turtles into the ocean.

Conservation Efforts

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery works to protect sea turtles in a variety of ways. They monitor and protect turtle nesting sites from poachers and predators, and release hatchlings into the sea. The hatchery also provides education programs for visitors and locals to raise awareness about turtle conservation. In addition, they conduct research to better understand the needs of sea turtles and how to protect them.

How You Can Help

You can support Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery and their efforts to protect sea turtles by visiting the hatchery and taking part in their education programs. You can also donate to the hatchery or purchase merchandise from their gift shop. In addition, you can help protect sea turtles by reducing your use of single-use plastics, properly disposing of waste, and supporting organizations that work to clean up beaches and oceans.
